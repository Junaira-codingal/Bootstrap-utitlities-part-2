The Dyatlov Pass Incident: A Hike Turned Into a Nightmare

In 1959, nine experienced hikers set out on an expedition in the Ural Mountains of Russia—but none returned alive. Their bodies were found in bizarre conditions: some had crushed bones, others were missing body parts, and no signs of struggle were found. Theories range from secret military experiments to UFO encounters, but the mystery remains unsolved to this day.
