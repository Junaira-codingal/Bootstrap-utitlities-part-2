The Toy-Box Killer: A Nightmare in the Desert

Whispers of the tortured still haunt the sands of New Mexico...

David Parker Ray, known as the "Toy-Box Killer," was a sadistic kidnapper and suspected serial killer who operated in New Mexico. He built a torture chamber, known as the "Toy Box," where he held and tormented his victims. Though he was convicted, the true number of his victims remains unknown.

Hidden in the New Mexico desert, Ray's trailer was a soundproofed chamber filled with chains, medical instruments, and recordings he played to his captives. Investigators believe he may have killed over 60 people, but no bodies were ever found.

While several women managed to escape, including Cynthia Vigil, many victims were never identified. The true scale of his crimes remains a chilling mystery.
