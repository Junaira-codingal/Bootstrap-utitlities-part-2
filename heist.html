D.B. Cooper: The Man Who Robbed the Skies and Disappeared Into Thin Air

On November 24, 1971, a man known as D.B. Cooper hijacked a Boeing 727, demanding $200,000 and four parachutes. After receiving the ransom, he jumped out of the plane mid-flight over Washington state and was never seen again. Despite an extensive FBI investigation, his true identity and fate remain unknown. In 1980, a small portion of the stolen money was found, but the rest is missing. Cooper’s heist remains one of history’s greatest unsolved mysteries.
